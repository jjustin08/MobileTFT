How to play:
1.Buy pawns
2.place pawns on battle arena
3.fight other teams

Getting 3 of same pawn will combine them

each pawn has different types
having multiple of same type will give bonus stats

leveling up will give you more pawns slot of board (max pawns = level)
and will show different pawns in the shop

Losing a fight will damage your player health

create different combinations of pawns for different winning strategies

Win by outlasting the other player // not in game yet
lose by losing all your player health

you can click on left side icons to get type info

you can click on pawns to get info
